Costs & Refund

How does refund work?
As a private practitioner, Dr. Demirel bills you directly. You can submit the invoice to your health insurer for reimbursement – depending on the insurer you typically receive up to 80% of the standard tariff back. On request, the invoice can be transmitted (electronically) to your insurer. If you have supplementary insurance with private practice cover, you may claim an additional portion from that insurer.

Fees: Costs for first visit, follow-up and procedures (e.g. ECG, echocardiography) are available on request when booking or by email.

Payment: Cash and card payment at the practice.

Cancellation: Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours’ notice may be charged a flat cancellation fee.

About

Research

Dr. Demirel’s research focus is on clinical outcome research in modern heart valve therapies. His work is published in internationally renowned journals such as:

  • European Heart Journal – Cardiovascular Imaging
  • JAMA Network Open
  • EuroIntervention
  • European Journal of Clinical Investigation

Research topics include:

  • Paravalvular leak after TAVI
  • Stroke risk in structural heart interventions
  • Long-term survival after transcatheter-based procedures
  • Frailty assessment using modern imaging
